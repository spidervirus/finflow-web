FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FinFlow

When will FinFlow be available?

We're currently in the final stages of development and plan to launch in Q3 2023. Join our waitlist to be notified when we go live and get exclusive early access offers.

Can I import data from other financial systems?

Yes, FinFlow supports importing data from CSV files and direct integration with popular accounting software like QuickBooks, Xero, and banking systems. Our import wizard makes the transition smooth and painless.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (256-bit AES) and security measures to ensure your data is always protected. We're also compliant with industry standards for financial data security including SOC 2, GDPR, and CCPA.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, all plans come with a 14-day free trial so you can explore FinFlow's features before committing. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can upgrade or cancel at any time.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Your billing will be prorated accordingly, so you'll only pay for what you use. There are no penalties for changing plans.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer different levels of support based on your plan. All customers receive email support, while Professional and Enterprise plans include priority support with faster response times. Enterprise customers also get a dedicated account manager.
